Frequently Asked Questions

"How do pre-orders work?" For some items, I will have a period where orders are open, where I collect all orders. Once the time is up, I place the orders to the manufacturer. Once all materials are received, I package and ship them out as fast as possible.

"Can I resell?" No. Trades and giveaways are OK though.

"What dropshipping service do you use?" Printify!

"How long does it take for you to pack and ship?" For regular items, I pack them right away, and shipping is done within 1-2 days. Shirts, hoodies and mugs take longer due to needing to drop-ship them.

"Can you ship internationally?" Yes! As long as USPS can ship it there, I can too.

"Where do you ship from?" I ship from America!
